#### 3. THE VERDICT

**Peer EV/Sales:** APP 22x, RBLX 7x, EA 6x, ADSK 7x, TTWO 4x → **median: ~7x** Excluding APP (outlier premium): **median ~6x**

Unity trades at ~5.4x forward EV/Sales vs peer median of **~6x** (ex-APP) = **~10% discount**, or ~23% below full peer median including AppLovin. [Seeking Alpha](https://seekingalpha.com/article/4882525-unity-stock-software-the-valuation-reset-ive-been-waiting-for)

**The math:**

- $U at 5.4x sales vs peer median 6x = **10% discount**
- $U strategic revenue growing **+35% YoY** vs EA (+12%) and ADSK (+17%), which trade at 6–7x EV/Sales — Unity is growing 2x faster at a cheaper multiple than those two
- AppLovin at 22x sales is growing 59% — APP is genuinely in a different tier and deserves premium

**Anomaly worth noting:** Unity's $347M GAAP net loss in Q1 was driven heavily by ironSource impairment and Supersonic write-downs, not operating deterioration — core adj. EBITDA margin expanded 800bps YoY to 27%. The GAAP noise masks improving unit economics. [ChartMill](https://www.chartmill.com/news/U/Chartmill-47711-Unity-Software-NYSEU-Surges-After-Q1-Earnings-Beat-But-GAAP-Loss-and-Guidance-Raise-Caution)

**Risk:** $2.2B in convertible notes matures 2026–2030. If GAAP profitability slips past Q4 2026, refinancing optionality narrows.
